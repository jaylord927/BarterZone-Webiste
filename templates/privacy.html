Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 30 2025

Introduction

At BarterZone, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our trading platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, full name, contact number
  • Profile Details: Location, birthdate, and other optional profile information
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged with other traders

Trading Information

  • Item Listings: Photos, descriptions, and details of items you list for trade
  • Trade History: Records of trade requests, acceptances, and completions
  • Arrangement Details: Delivery addresses, meetup locations, and exchange methods

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience

How We Use Your Information

Platform Operation

To create and maintain your account, facilitate trades, and enable communication between users

Safety & Security

To monitor for fraudulent activity, enforce our terms of service, and protect our community

Improvements

To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience through platform improvements

Communication

To send important updates about your trades, account, and platform changes

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username, item listings, and trade-related information are visible to other BarterZone members
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted partners who help us operate the platform (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze how users interact with our platform
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and features

Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect some platform features.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
